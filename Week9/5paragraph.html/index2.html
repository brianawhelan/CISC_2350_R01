The summer of 2017 was one for the books. I absolutely love to travel and see the world. I was forturante enough to travel to so many amazing places. One of my favorite places was Montreal, Quebec.

There is something beautiful, artistic, ancient, and modern to find everywhere you go around Montreal. From old gothic arcitecture to vibrant colored city streets you are bound to fall in love with this city.

Montreal is home to the beautiful Notre Dame Bascilica. The Notre Dame Bascilica of Montreal was unbelievable. It's interior design is truly iconic and a must see if you ever have the chance to visit. The Mount Royal Park was absolutley breathtaking with amazing views of the whole city. The park lies on highest point of the city. Fun Fact the designer of New York's very own Central Park, Fredrick Law Olmstead also designed Mont Royal. I can't wait to go back..

Montreal is considered one of the most creative cities in the world. Beautiful street art and murals flourish throughout the whole city. Infact you find more than 1,000 paintings small or large around the city.

And lastly, how can I forget the food? Montreal is not only the the French-speaking metropolis in North America, Montreal is a cultural junction. From their infamous poutine to fine French cuisine, I may of gained a hundred pounds in those 3 short days..
