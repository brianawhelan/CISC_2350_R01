What is the Collision Cam?

The Collision Cam is 360 bird view system built into your vechile. The 360 degree car security camera, AKA the Collision Cam. The Collision Cam is driving and parking aid car camera system with video recording and CCD quality. The camera will bea feautred in all 2020 vechiles and will be installed in the Left, Right, Front, and Rear view cameras.

What does the camera do? First off, you have the option of leaving the camera on while driving or parked. Say if you decided to keep your camera on at all times, the Collision Cam will record your vechile's surroundings 24/7. When your car is impacted by another vechile the Collision Cam will instantly begin to take pictures of the other vechile and it's license plate number. However, if you choose to keep your camera off the Collision Cam will instantly take live videos and pictures of the other car once impacted.
