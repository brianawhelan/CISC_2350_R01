Description

The Collision Cam app is designed to essentially provide live footage of your car's surrondings. The Collision Cam app automatically captures every driving moment using the auto-loop technology and collision detection. The Collision Cam and it's app provide a dependable "silent witness" by your side.

App Features:

  • Live footage of your car's surrondings
  • Languages: English, French, German, Spanish
  • Display time/speed (metric and English units) on video
  • Camera on and off option
  • Live Photographs of incidents when detected by impact
  • Record video with resolutions low/medium/high/480/720
  • Record with or without audio
  • Auto record video when detecting an impact
  • Continues loop recording. Older videos are overridden by newer ones to preserve space on the device
  • Store recorded videos within the app or upload to the clouds (Google Drive/Drop Box/Box Drive)
  • Share video via email
